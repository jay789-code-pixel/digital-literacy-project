
# 🛡️ Stay Safe Online — Checklist for College Students

## ✅ The Checklist

###  Account & Password Safety

-  **Use a strong password** — mix letters, numbers & symbols. `Jay@2025` is better than `jay123`
-  **Don't use the same password** for Instagram, Gmail, and your bank. If one gets hacked, all get hacked.
-  **Turn on 2-Step Verification** on Gmail, Instagram, and WhatsApp. It takes 2 minutes and saves you a lot of pain.

---

###  Social Media & Personal Info

-  **Don't post your phone number or address** publicly on Instagram bios or college groups. Strangers are watching.
-  **Think before you share** — once something is online, it's online forever. Screenshots exist.
-  **Be careful with "free gift" links** sent on WhatsApp. If it looks too good to be true, it's a trap.

---

###  UPI & Financial Safety *(Important!)*

-  **Never share your UPI PIN or OTP with anyone** — not even someone who claims to be from your bank or Google Pay support. Real support staff never ask for this.
-  **"Receive money" requests don't need a PIN** — if someone says "enter your PIN to receive ₹500", they are stealing from you, not sending you money.

---

###  Wi-Fi & Device Safety

-  **Avoid using public Wi-Fi** (college canteen, bus stand, cafe) for banking or logging into important accounts. Use mobile data instead.
-  **Lock your phone** with a PIN or fingerprint. If you lose it unlocked, everything is gone.

---
